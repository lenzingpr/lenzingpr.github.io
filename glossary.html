Coverage Type Definition
"Online - Press Release" and "Print - Press Release"
  • Refers to coverage that publishes the press release (including all reposting of press release and/or from newswire)
  • Any coverage published via PRN (i.e. press release or insight article) will also be counted under this coverage type
"Online - Interview” and “Print - Interview” Refers to coverage generated from media interviews
“Online - Byline” and “Print - Byline” Refers to published byline articles
“Online – Original” and “Print – Original” Refers to original articles drafted by media based on Lenzing content. If an article appears repeatedly across different media outlets: Only the first coverage will be counted as “Original article”, others will be counted as reposting.
“Online - Reposting (exclude press release)” 'Refers to all types of reposting articles excluding press release and PRN content
“Broadcast” Refers to video coverage
